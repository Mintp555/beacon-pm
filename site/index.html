Why pursue CARF accreditation
A recognised signal of quality — and a driver of real organisational improvement.

CARF accreditation demonstrates conformance to internationally accepted standards in health and human services. Beyond the certificate, the process itself strengthens how an organisation is governed, managed, and improved — which is why it is worth running as a serious project.
