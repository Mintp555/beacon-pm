Core service areas
01
CARF Readiness Review

A focused assessment of where the organisation stands against CARF standards — identifying gaps, risks, and priorities before committing to full implementation.

  • Leadership interview and scoping
  • Gap assessment against CARF standards
  • Risk identification and prioritisation
  • Implementation roadmap and timeline
  • Governance and ownership recommendations
02
Project Management for CARF Implementation

End-to-end project management of the implementation — coordinating departments, tracking documentation, and keeping leadership informed with clear, measurable progress toward survey readiness.

  • Implementation roadmap and milestones
  • Department ownership matrix
  • Documentation tracking and control
  • Risk management and escalation
  • Leadership alignment
03
Operational Improvement & Digital Project Delivery

Project management support for operational improvement initiatives including healthcare technology, workflow optimisation, digital transformation, and software implementation.

Healthcare software implementation IT infrastructure projects Web platform delivery API integrations Automation workflows UAT & QA coordination
